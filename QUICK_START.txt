# 🚀 NutriTracker - QUICK START GUIDE

## ⚡ Get Started in 60 Seconds

### Step 1: Access the App
Open your browser and go to:
```
http://localhost:5000
```

### Step 2: Create Account
- Click "Sign Up"
- Enter username, email, password
- Click "Create Account"

### Step 3: Login
- Enter your credentials
- Click "Login"

### Step 4: Update Profile
- Click "👤 Profile" → "✏️ Edit Profile"
- Enter: Age, Weight (kg), Height (cm), Gender, Activity Level, Goal
- Click "Save Changes"
- System shows your BMI and daily calorie target

### Step 5: Log Your First Meal
- Click "🍽️ Meals"
- Click "Add a New Meal"
- Select meal type and date
- Search for food items
- Add to meal
- View nutrition totals

### Step 6: Check Progress
- Click "📈 Progress"
- View daily nutrition summary
- Set fitness goals
- Monitor weekly progress
